Product Overview

Draw Wire Sensors

  • ranges 50 mm - 42.5 m
  • linearity up to ±0.02 %
  • resolution up to 17 µm
  • output: potentiometric, analog, digital
    incremental, digital absolute,  Bus, tachometer

Lasersensors

  • ranges 0.5 mm - 150 m
  • linearity up to 1 µm
  • resolution up to 0.2 µm
  • triangulation or optical phase comparison

Linearpotentiometer

  • ranges 10 - 2000 mm
  • linearity up to ±0.05%
  • output: potentiometric, analog
  • protection class up to IP67

Magnetic Scales

  • Measurement range 0 - 100 m
  • Linearity up to ±15 μm
  • Accuracy up to 10 μm
  • Output: Digital - Incremental
  • with and without reference point

Digital Length Gauges

  • Measurement range up to 25 mm
  • Linearity up to ±0,2 μm
  • Resolution up to 0,05 µm
  • Output: 1 Vss, TTL 1 Vss, TTL

Ultrasonic Sensors

  • ranges 100 - 6000 mm
  • linearity up to ±0.3 %
  • resolution up to 0.25 mm
  • output: analog, proximity switch
  • ATEX version available

Magnetostrictive Transducers

  • ranges 50 - 4000 mm
  • linearity up to ±0.02 %
  • resolution up to 2 µm
  • output: analog, SSI, CANopen, tachometer

Digital Scales

  • Measurement range up to 2000 mm
  • Resolution max. 5 μm
  • Linearity up to ± 30 μm
  • Output: digital incremental TTL / HTL

Capacitive Sensors

  • ranges 0.05 - 10 mm
  • linearity up to ± 0.2 %
  • resolution dynamic 0.01 %
  • output: analog
  • Working temp: -50...+200°C

Encoders & Rotary Transducers

  • singleturn and multiturn encoders
  • analog and digital transducer
  • solid shaft, hollow shaft, through hollow shaft
  • output: potentiometric, analog, digital incremental, digital absolute, Bus
  • rotational measurement

Hydraulic Sensors

  • Hydraulic Transducer SX200 / SX300
  • Magnetostrictive Transducer MSB / MOP / MAZ
  • Linearpotentiometer LMI12 / LMI12-SE

Signal Conditioner

  • DIN rail mounting
  • converts potentiometer signals into analog output signals
  • Input: 1...20 kOhm
  • with cable break detection

Display Controls

  • multifunctional display
  • input: analog/digital
  • 1 - 2 channels
  • Scaling and linearisation functions
  • output: analog, USB, RS232, RS485 and more
